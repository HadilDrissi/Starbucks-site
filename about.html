Our History

Starbucks is a global coffee chain founded in 1971 in Seattle, Washington, USA.

It initially sold only coffee beans and coffee-making equipment. In the 1980s, it began serving freshly brewed coffee and expanded rapidly, eventually becoming a global brand with thousands of locations worldwide. Today, Starbucks is known for its wide range of coffee beverages, pastries, and other food items, as well as its commitment to sustainability and social responsibility.
